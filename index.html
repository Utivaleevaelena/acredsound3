My Story

I know what it feels like
to be out of alignment

After years in healthcare risk management, I understood stress, loss, and burnout from the inside. I saw firsthand how the pace of everyday life can quietly disconnect us from our own inner wisdom, peace, and sense of self.

My healing journey began with Reiki, but over time it evolved into something much deeper. As I've grown into my gifts and continued my training, I've learned to weave together multiple healing traditions and practices. One of the most meaningful gifts I carry is the wisdom passed down through my grandmother, including traditional egg energy cleansing practices used to support energetic clearing and spiritual renewal.

My work is also influenced by earth-based healing traditions, honoring the natural tools and elements that have been used for generations to support healing, reflection, and connection. Whether through Reiki, sound healing, energy clearing, or intuitive guidance, I approach each session with deep respect for both the sacred and the practical.

At the heart of everything I do is my faith. Before every session, I pray and ask God to guide me, use me as a channel for healing, and show me what is needed for the highest good of the person in front of me. I believe true healing comes not from me, but through me.

Every session is rooted in compassion, reverence, and respect for the mind–body–spirit connection. My role is simply to hold space for your journey, helping you release what no longer serves you, reconnect with your inner wisdom, and remember the peace, strength, and wholeness that have always been within you.

Cacao
Ceremonial Cacao
$30 Add-on $25

Used for centuries by Indigenous cultures of Central and South America in heart-opening rituals, meditation, and community gatherings. Unlike regular hot chocolate, ceremonial cacao is minimally processed and prepared intentionally to support emotional connection and inner reflection.

Cacao may help promote relaxation, emotional openness, creativity, and a deeper connection to yourself — a gentle sense of warmth, grounding, and clarity.

Perfect for those wanting to slow down, soften, and enter their healing with an open heart.

Blue Lotus
Blue Lotus
Tea Ceremony
$25 Add-on $20

Rooted in ancient Egyptian traditions, where it was used in spiritual ceremonies, meditation, and sacred rituals. Known as a calming botanical, blue lotus has long been associated with relaxation, intuition, and gentle emotional release.

This tea may help support a peaceful state of mind, deepen meditation, and encourage nervous system relaxation. Many describe the experience as dreamy, soothing, and grounding.

Creates space to unwind, reconnect inward, and settle into stillness before or after your session.

Kava
Kava Ceremony
$20 Add-on $15

Traditionally used throughout the South Pacific for centuries in community gatherings and ceremonial practices to promote relaxation, connection, and peace.

Kava may help calm the nervous system, ease tension, and support emotional relaxation without mental fog — a beautiful addition for those seeking grounding and stress relief before a session.

Please note: Kava can cause drowsiness or deep relaxation. For safety, it is not recommended for clients who are driving themselves immediately afterward.
